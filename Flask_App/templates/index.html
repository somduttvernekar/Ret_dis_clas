What is RetiScan?

RetiScan identifies multiple diseases such as Diabetic retinopathy (DR), Media Haze (MH), Optic disc cupping (ODC) using retinal fundus images and displays the results to the user.

Why use our application?

Retinal imaging allows eye doctors to see signs of eye diseases that they could not see before. The test itself is painless and the results are easy for doctors to interpret. Our system analyses your retinal images and gives you accurate predicted results. Your doctor can store the images on a computer and compare them with other scans.

How does it work?

This application uses a Deep Learning approach in which the processed images will be fed into a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) to predict whether the patient is suffering from the disease.
